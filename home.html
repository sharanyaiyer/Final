Diagnostics

'Diagnostics Module' compares and analysis trends for an activity across multiple countries over different time period.

  • Partitions data for an activity into three different time window and analyzes trends across three different windows.
  • Modules help to understand trends and spends / Macro activites and help in planning for subsequent quarters.

Event Management

'Event Management Module' is a library for all the activites that are planned, users can also input competitive activities.

  • This module helps users track their previous Campaigns.
  • Any activity self or competitor can be plotted on the charts in the Diagnostics Module.
  • Helps in understanding import of sponsorships, wether and other activities.

Simulator

'Simulator Module' capabilities of convergytics MMX solutions aids the decision process by estimating the following.

  • Incremental Volume Contribution: sales volume directly attributable to a specific marketing activity.
  • "Due-To's": Incremental Volume from each activity over the previous year.

Then manager can then decide to switch investments between activities, brands, attributes and across time to optimize marketing budgets.
